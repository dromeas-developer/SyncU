Features

View your Health Connect data alongside Intervals.icu data. See what's synced and what's not, all in one place.

🔄 Automatic Sync

Enable twice-daily automatic syncing. Set it once and your data stays up to date without any effort.

⚡ Manual Control

Pull to refresh Health Connect data. Tap to sync specific days. Full control when you need it.

🔒 Privacy First

Your data goes directly from your device to Intervals.icu. We don't store or access anything. Ever.

📱 Simple & Clean

No complicated settings. No confusing menus. Just your data, clearly displayed and easily synced.

🆓 Free & Open Source

No ads. No tracking. No premium features. Completely free and open source on GitHub.

How It Works

1

Connect

Grant Health Connect permissions and add your Intervals.icu API credentials

2

Compare

View your data side-by-side. See what's in Health Connect vs what's in Intervals.icu

3

Sync

Enable auto-sync or manually sync specific days. Your data flows directly to Intervals.icu

What Gets Synced

  • ✅ Activities (running, cycling, walking, etc.)
  • ✅ Heart metrics (resting HR, average, HRV)
  • ✅ Steps & distance
  • ✅ Calories burned
  • ✅ Sleep (duration + stages)
  • ✅ Body metrics (weight, body fat)
